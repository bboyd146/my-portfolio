About me

Most of my hobbies include something that has to do with food and drinks, but one of my recently found hobbies is riding Motorcycles. About a few months ago, I test rode one of my brother's motorcycles and fell in love instantly. I bought one of my own not too long ago and plan on adding to my inventory as the years go by. My work history doesn't include any software development, but my schooling on the other hand does. I've always wanted to be an Electrical Engineer, so most of my course work from senior high through college was geared as so. I've worked on projects building landscapes in AutoCAD, built and produced movement codes for small robots to perform minor tasks, and learned some basics of C++ programming language. After successfully completing my term at Rice University's Online Coding Bootcamp and receiving my certificate, I plan on continuing my growth in the Software development field.
